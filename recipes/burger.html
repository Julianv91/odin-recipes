BORGIR

A borgir is a masterpiece of a dish

Ingredients

  • Borgir meat
  • Borir bread
  • Pickles x10
  • Borgrir dressing

How to make

  1. Mash borgir meat into round patty
  2. Fry on high heat in pan untill done
  3. Fry borgir bread in pan after
  4. Add borgir meat to borgir bread with dressing and pickles
  5. Done
